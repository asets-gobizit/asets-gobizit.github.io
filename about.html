About Danny Nissani, Attorney

I provide legal services for Israelis and Hebrew speakers living abroad, with a focus on clarity, risk awareness, and practical solutions.

My work is centered on contracts, agreements, and legal translation – helping you understand what you are signing, what can go wrong, and how to protect your interests in a foreign legal environment.

How I work

My approach is straightforward:

  • Plain language explanations, not legal jargon.
  • Focusing on real risks and practical choices, not theory.
  • Respecting your time and budget – we prioritize what really matters.
  • Working transparently so you always know where things stand.

Who I typically help

I often work with:

  • Hebrew-speaking families and individuals living in Spain or Europe.
  • Entrepreneurs and small business owners signing contracts abroad.
  • People who receive Spanish or English documents and want them explained in Hebrew.
